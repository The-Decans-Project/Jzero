### How to Stay Compliant

#### Step 1: Review Swiss Terms
Visit: https://www.astro.com/swisseph/

#### Step 2: Determine Your Use Case
- **Personal hobby project?** → Free
- **Selling astrology services?** → Requires Swiss license
- **Embedded in commercial app?** → Requires Swiss license
- **Publishing astrology content?** → Check with Swiss

#### Step 3: For Commercial Use
1. Visit https://www.astro.com/swisseph/
2. Review their license options
3. Contact Astodienst if unsure
4. Purchase appropriate license
5. Follow their licensing terms

#### Step 4: Dual Compliance
When using Swiss commercially:

```
Your Obligations:
├── Follow Jzero MIT License (code you write)
├── Follow Swiss Ephemeris License (ephemeris data)
└── Include both attributions in your product
```
